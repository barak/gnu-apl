The characters used in the interpreter.

Macro char_def( , xx) defines characters in ⎕AV which have APL relevance.

Macro char_def( , 0xx) defines characters in ⎕AV without APL relevance. These characters come from the IBM APL2 Workstation ⎕AV and are subject to being removed in favour of new APL characters with APL relevance.

Macro char_uni() defines characters not in ⎕AV, have no APL relevance, but were declared as members UNI_xxx in enum Unicode so that they can be referenced with name in the C++ source files.
