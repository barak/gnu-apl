◆ Svar_Control

enum Svar_Control

shared variables control (what is NOT allowed). Terminology: _OFF: the parner that has first offered the variable, _ACC: the parner that has accepted the offer _1: the partner reading ot changing the control vector _2: the other partner of _1 (if any)

Normally the APL interpreter is _1 == _OFF and the partner is _2 == _ACC. If not (eg. when the partner offers first) then the functions set_control() and get_control() map the _1 and _2 arg (of set_control()) and result (of get_control()) to the proper _OFF and _ACC int in in the flags of the corresponding partner.
