Detailed Description

One item in a CollatingCache.

one item of a CollatingCache. Every character in A (of A⍋B or A⍒B gets one CollatingCacheEntry; if the same character occurs multiple times in A, then the first one (in row-major order) creates the entry and the remaining copies of the character use the same CollatingCacheEntry.

Later on, f two Unicodes c1 and c2 shall be compared, then the ce_shapes (which are actually indices into A) of the CollatingCacheEntry for c1 and c2 are used for the comparison and the lowest dimansion wins.
